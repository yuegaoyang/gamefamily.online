Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this action-packed adventure!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival action game where you face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating enemies and collecting power-ups.

With its engaging gameplay and progressive difficulty, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for players of all skill levels.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Attacks are automatic when enemies are in range
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities and upgrades when leveling up

Tips

  • Stay constantly moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize collecting experience to level up quickly
  • Balance offensive and defensive abilities
  • Plan your upgrades based on your play style
